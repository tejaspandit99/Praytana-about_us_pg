About Us


प्रyatna is an initiative under Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya (SVVV), Indore which aims at providing quality education to the students of government schools through interactive medium. प्रyatna is a Non Profitable Organization run and managed by the students and faculties of SVVV who believe that Communication Skills and Computer Education are the two important aspects of education which cannot be overlooked. प्रyatna wishes to work with the teachers at government schools to gain the valuable experience from them and boost the social serving agenda of the NPO. Also, this will inculcate in our volunteers the feeling of giving back to the society. We focus on teaching English and Computer Education through interactive and creative mediums so as to infuse the knowledge of these vital subjects in students. Additionally, we also try to give the students -artistic knowledge, general knowledge, and awareness. Main aim of प्रyatna is to give children enough idea of possible career paths they can pursue in the future, and to be able to live a bright future.

Our Vision

Education is the most powerful weapon that you can use to change the World. We believe that if you change a child’s life, you change a family’s which can change a community and ultimately the world.
We want to be the light over darkness. The idea is to share what we have "SHIKSHA". And in the process, inspire people around us to give back to those who need it most.
